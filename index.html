About

Hi, I'm Arthur, a second year mathematics undergraduate at Trinity College, Cambridge. I hope to gather here some things related to the maths tripos and some more things of general interest.

Tripos

Optimization (source) - a (currently incomplete) set of notes intended to provide more of a background to a course I think is extremely underrated.

Other recommended resources

  • Intuition-focused second year notes by Neel Nanda. Some also available for third year courses, too.
  • The mathmo guide of almost 1000 reviews of tripos courses. Contribute your own, save a puremo from attending a physics course!
  • General notes by David Bai and Qiangru Kuang - not as well known as Dexter's, but often more up to date (and better!).

Competitive Programming

I'm actively involved with programming competitions for both school and university students. If you're interested, take a look at the beginner's events the UCCPS has put on (including an interactive workshop and several tailor-made contests). I also regularly compete in contests on Codeforces and AtCoder (both sites have ELO-like rating systems, and are recommended).

Recommended Reading

  • Neural networks and deep learning
